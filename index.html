About me

Hi! My name is Montserrat (mon-se-rat). I am a senior electrical engineering student at UCF. I began attending Fall 2019 and expect to graduate by Spring 2024. So far, I have had the opportunity to participate in a small research project in which I learned important skills by helping to solder, build, and program simple haptic feedback circuits. Additionally, I interned at a small local company where I gained extensive experience using EAGLE and got to see more of how PCBs are manufactured, from hardware to software. Through my coursework and hobbies, I have come to also develop a strong interest and passion for programming. In my free time, I love to draw, make websites, and rollerskate.

About Me

I am currently a junior electrical engineering student at the University of Central Florida, located in Orlando. I began attending Fall 2019 and expect to graduate by Fall 2023. Throughout my time at UCF, I have been involved with my school's IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) chapter and undergraduate research. My participation in extrac has helped me find a passion for mechatronics and embedded systems. During my free time, I create all sorts of art including character sketches and typography videos.
